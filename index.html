Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation is the process of creating something new or improving upon existing ideas, products, or services.

Sustainability

Sustainability focuses on meeting present needs without compromising the ability of future generations to 1 meet their own needs.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure refers to the fundamental systems and facilities that support the operations of a society or enterprise.

Empowerment

Empowerment is the process of increasing an individual's or group's autonomy, self-confidence, and capacity to make choices.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Digital Transformation is the integration of digital technology into all areas of a business, fundamentally changing how it operates and delivers value.

Green Energy

Green energy refers to renewable energy sources like solar, wind, and hydro power, which have minimal environmental impact.

Modern Infrastructure

Modern infrastructure emphasizes sustainability, resilience, and technological integration, ensuring efficient and adaptable systems for the future.
